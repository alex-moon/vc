vc is open source now!

AJ Moon
16 November, 2021

My original intention with vc was to open source it. Before I did that, however, I wanted to see what potential there was to get rich making NFTs of the finished products. Now that I've had a glimpse of what it would take to get rich that way, I'm satisfied I have enough evidence to make a conclusion, viz.: it sucks and I hate it.

I am, therefore, proud to announce that, effective immediately, I'm open sourcing the vc codebase. It's now public on Github.
