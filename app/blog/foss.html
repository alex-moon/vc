vc is open source now!

My original intention with vc was to open source it. Before I did that, however, I wanted to see what potential there was to get rich off the finished products by doing NFTs. I have now had enough experience of what it would take to make a satisfactory conclusion, viz. that it sucks and I hate it.

I am, therefore, effective immediately, open sourcing the vc codebase.
